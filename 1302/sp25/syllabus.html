Academic Honesty Policy:

All programming assignments must be original work of the student. Copying of code from the internet, AI, or from other sources is strictly prohibited. If the work is determined to be plagiarized, a 0 will be awarded for the assignment. Determination of plagiarism will be made by the instructor. No outside help is permitted.
